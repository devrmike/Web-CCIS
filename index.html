Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Is the event open to all programs?
    Yes! While it is organized by CCIS, elements like the showcase are open to all campus enthusiasts.
  • 2. Do I need to register early?
    Yes, slots for hackathons and specific labs are limited due to physical computer terminals.
